General Rules

No NLR If you have died, you must wait 5 minutes before you can return to the place of death. Breaking this rule will result in a warn.
No RDM If you kill a person without a reason that pertains to roleplay, you will receive a warn.
No loopholing If you do something that is obviously with intent to bend the rules, you will receive a warn and/or ban.
Use Common Sense If you do something that is obviously done malicious intent or to troll, you will be warned and/or banned.

Basing Rules

Fading Doors You can use as many fading doors as you see fit as long as it is within the server's limit.
One Way Windows You cannot shoot at an intruder without them being able to clearly view you.
Obstacles: If you have obstacles, the restrictions go as follows: You must be able to crouch under obstacles in front of you (excluding fading doors), and you must be able to walk without any prop interference

Trading and Gambling

No scamming You may not do anything that does not allow the receiving player to gain any goods for their money spent.
No Out-Of-Game Transactions If you try to trade for items in-game without using the proper channels, you will be permanently banned on the spot. SlopRP does not take any responsibility for money lost out-of-game.
